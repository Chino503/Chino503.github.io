Introduction to Chemistry: Objective 1

Chemistry: the study of the composition of matter and changes that matter undergoes.

Matter – Anything that has volume or mass.
What is NOT matter?
Light, heat, radio waves and magnetic fields.

Scientific Method – A systematic approach for learning about the world.

  1. Observe an event or process. States the problem and gathers background information.
  2. Form a hypothesis – a suggested explenationfor observed events.
  3. Perform experiments test the hypothesis.
    • Variables factor that may change.
      • Independent Variable (manipulated variable)
        • change only one variable
        • Ex. Amount of fertilizer.
      • Dependent Variable (responding variable)
        • what you measure during experiment
        • Ex. How high theplant grows
    • Control setup: do something to it.
    • Multiple trials ensure accuracy
  4. Record and analize data.
    • Data – recorded observation and measurement.
    • Create a data table and/or graph
    • Analize – make a calculation.
  5. Draw conclusions
    • create a theory to explain the result
